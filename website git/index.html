Our Farm Story

Three generations of sustainable grain farming

Aschalew Kassaye - Founder & Lead Farmer

Founded in 2001, Aschalew Kassaye Farm has grown from a small family operation to a respected producer of premium wheat and barley. Our commitment to sustainable practices and quality has never wavered.

We believe in responsible farming that nourishes both people and the land. Our grains are grown without synthetic pesticides, using crop rotation and natural soil enrichment techniques.
